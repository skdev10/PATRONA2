Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find a specific breed?

Simply use our breed search feature by selecting categories like country, size, or behavior type to narrow down the options.

Where do the photos come from?

The images are sourced from reputable animal photographers and partners who have generously allowed us to use their work. All credits are included on each photo.

Can I submit my dog's photo?

Yes! If you'd like to contribute, please use our 'Submit a Photo' section on the main website or contact us via email.
